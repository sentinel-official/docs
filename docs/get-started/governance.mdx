Sentinel uses on-chain governance, meaning anyone who stakes P2P can submit proposals and vote on them. Proposals can cover anything from protocol upgrades to funding allocations to parameter changes.

## Voting

Staked P2P holders can browse and vote on active proposals through a block explorer like [this one](https://explorer.busurnode.com/sentinel/gov). Each vote is recorded on-chain and weighted by the amount of P2P staked.

Proposal discussion happens on [Common](https://gov.sentinel.co/sentinel/community-home), where the community can share context, rationale, and feedback before and during voting.

![](/img/get-started/voting.png)

## Creating Proposals

You can create and manage governance proposals through the Sentinel Hub CLI. The available commands are documented in the [governance commands](/sentinel-hub/commands/governance) section.
